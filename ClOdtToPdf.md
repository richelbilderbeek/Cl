([Command line](Cl.htm)) [convert ODT to PDF](ClOdtToPdf.htm)
=============================================================

 

[convert ODT to PDF](ClOdtToPdf.htm) is a [command line](Cl.htm) snippet
to convert an ODT document to a PDF document.

 

  -----------------------------------
  ` unoconv -f pdf my_odt_file.odt`
