([Command line](Cl.htm)) [convert BMP to PNG](ClBmpToPng.htm)
=============================================================

 

 

 

 

 

All files in a folder at once:

 

  -------------------------------------------------------------------------
  ``  for x in *.bmp; do convert "$x" "`basename "$x" .bmp`.png"; done ``
  -------------------------------------------------------------------------
